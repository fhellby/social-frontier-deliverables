# Social Frontier — Hypotheses (Falsifiable Community Bets)

> Hypotheses are the directional bets the community is collectively making.
> Each must be falsifiable: it specifies what evidence would prove it wrong.
> Velocity: 🟢 strengthening | 🟡 holding | 🟠 weakening | 🔴 contested/falsified

As-of: 2026-02-26

---

### H1 — "OSS Agentic Dev Stack Reaches Production Adoption by Q4 2026"
**Domain:** AI/Dev Tools
**Claim:** By Q4 2026, at least one open-source agentic development environment will reach production adoption: ≥10k GitHub stars AND confirmed deployment by ≥3 independent teams not affiliated with the creator.
**Time horizon:** Q4 2026 (Oct–Dec)

**Falsification conditions:**
- No OSS agentic env reaches 10k stars by Q4 2026
- OR the leading candidate (Emdash/Pi/similar) is acquired and closed-sourced
- OR production teams report agentic envs are "interesting but not production-ready" by Q4

**Evidence supporting:** Pi [HN 416], Emdash [HN 167], HuggingFace Skills [HN 175], Computer Action Model [HN 165] — four independent convergent efforts in one week. Stack is crystallizing.
**Evidence against:** All current tools are pre-1.0. "Production-ready" has consistently been 2-4 years away in AI tooling.
**Velocity:** 🟢 strengthening

### H2 — "Anthropic Disrupts ≥3 Legacy IT Verticals by End of 2026"
**Domain:** AI/Startups
**Claim:** Following the COBOL/IBM play, Anthropic will execute targeted vertical disruption against at least 3 separate legacy IT revenue streams by Dec 2026, each causing ≥5% stock impact on the targeted incumbent or displacing ≥$100M ARR.
**Time horizon:** Dec 2026

**Falsification conditions:**
- COBOL tool turns out to be a one-off product, not a strategic pattern
- No second targeted vertical disruption announced by June 2026
- Anthropic pivots to competing on general benchmarks instead

**Evidence supporting:** Anthropic drops safety pledge (commercial pressure signal), COBOL→IBM -10% establishes the playbook, N5 (Vertical AI > Horizontal) pattern strengthening.
**Evidence against:** COBOL tool may be one-off partnership; dropping safety pledge suggests internal prioritization chaos, not strategic clarity.
**Velocity:** 🟢 strengthening — but safety pledge drop introduces uncertainty

### H3 — "OSS AI Wins Majority Market Share in STT by H1 2026"
**Domain:** AI/Dev Tools
**Claim:** By June 2026, open-weight STT (speech-to-text) models will have majority usage share (>50% of developer-reported production deployments) vs. closed alternatives (OpenAI Whisper API, Google Speech, AWS Transcribe).
**Time horizon:** H1 2026 (June)

**Falsification conditions:**
- Survey/benchmark data shows closed models maintain >50% developer share by June 2026
- Moonshine performance claims are not reproducible on diverse accents/conditions
- Latency/infrastructure costs make OSS STT impractical for most production use cases

**Evidence supporting:** Moonshine beats WhisperLargeV3 [HN 263] — first open model to claim AND demonstrate benchmark parity in STT. Danish government OSS shift is institutional validation.
**Evidence against:** Benchmark accuracy ≠ production accuracy across accents, languages, noise conditions. WhisperLargeV3 has deep ecosystem integrations.
**Velocity:** 🟢 strengthening — single strong signal but high confidence

### H4 — "Developer Identity Crisis Produces New 'AI Craft' Movement by Mid-2026"
**Domain:** Dev Culture
**Claim:** By June 2026, a coherent "AI Craft" movement emerges — defined as a named, organized community of developers who explicitly position themselves as combining AI assistance with human craftsmanship principles. Indicators: named identity, manifesto-level content reaching 1k+ engagement.
**Time horizon:** June 2026

**Falsification conditions:**
- No coherent named identity emerges (individual complaints remain fragmented, no organizing principle)
- The backlash fades as developers adapt to AI tools without identity-level conflict
- The "movement" that emerges is anti-AI, not craft-positive

**Evidence supporting:** "AI has taken fun out of programming" [r/webdev 413, 359 comments] — identity layer, not technical layer. RAGS RFC = early movement indicator. "Craft preservationist" camp in T4 is crystallizing.
**Evidence against:** Developer communities historically don't organize into named identity movements easily. Individual complaints often dissipate without organizing.
**Velocity:** 🟡 holding — signals present but no organizing event yet

### H5 — "Vertical AI Startups Show 5× Faster Time-to-Profitability than Horizontal"
**Domain:** Startups/VC
**Claim:** By Dec 2026, publicly available case studies will show vertical AI startups (targeting one specific industry/workflow) reaching profitability in ≤12 months at a rate ≥3× higher than horizontal AI platform startups launched in the same period.
**Time horizon:** Dec 2026

**Falsification conditions:**
- No aggregated data emerges comparing vertical vs. horizontal startup trajectories
- Horizontal AI platforms (OpenAI API wrappers, etc.) demonstrate similar time-to-profitability rates
- The "vertical wins" examples turn out to be selection bias (only successes shared publicly)

**Evidence supporting:** Pediatric dental SaaS $4k→$22k in 8 months [r/Entrepreneur 399], eBay vertical clone exit, COBOL disruption pattern. Multiple independent data points in same week.
**Evidence against:** All evidence is anecdotal/self-reported. VC data on vertical vs. horizontal is not yet public. Survivorship bias is likely.
**Velocity:** 🟡 holding — strong anecdotal evidence, no aggregate data yet
